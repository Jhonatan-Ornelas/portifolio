FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase a product on your website and is it secure?

Buying from our website is easy and secure. First, use the search bar at the top of our homepage to look for a specific product or browse through our various product categories. When you find a product you're interested in, click on it to view more details. If you decide to purchase it, click the "Add to Cart" button. You can continue shopping and add more items to your cart, or you can click on the cart icon in the top right corner of the page to review the contents of your cart. When you're ready to proceed with the purchase, click "Checkout". You will be prompted to provide your shipping information and choose a payment method. We accept most major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express, as well as PayPal. After confirming your order, you will receive a confirmation email. As for security, we take the protection of your data very seriously. All transactions are encrypted using SSL technology, which is the industry standard for secure online transactions. We never store your credit card details on our servers.

How does shipping and delivery of the products work?

We strive to ship your order as quickly as possible. After your order is confirmed, the processing time typically takes 1 to 3 business days. Once shipped, the delivery time depends on the shipping method chosen during checkout and your delivery location. We offer various shipping options, ranging from standard delivery to express options. Shipping costs will be calculated and displayed during checkout. For orders above a certain value, we also offer free shipping. To track your order, you can use the tracking number provided in the shipping confirmation email. Please note that it may take a few days for the tracking information to be updated. If you have any questions about shipping or the delivery of your order, please don't hesitate to contact us.

How do returns and refunds work?

We strive to ensure that you are completely satisfied with your purchase. However, if for any reason you are not satisfied, you may return the item to us within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. To initiate a return, please contact us through our customer service. We will provide detailed instructions on how to return the item. Please note that the item must be returned in its original condition, including all packaging and accessories. Once we receive the returned item and confirm its condition, we will process your refund. The refund will be made using the same payment method you used in the original purchase and may take a few days to reflect in your account. If you have any questions or concerns about returns and refunds, please don't hesitate to contact us.

What are the main policies of the company?

We value our customers and have several policies in place to ensure that your shopping experience with us is positive. These include our Privacy Policy, which describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information; our Returns Policy, which allows you to return items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund; and our Shipping Policy, which details our shipping options and delivery times. Additionally, we have a GDPR Compliance Policy for customers in the European Union, and a Security Policy to ensure the protection of your credit card details and other sensitive information. All of our policies are available for review on our website. If you have any questions or concerns about any of our policies, please don't hesitate to contact us.

How can I contact Customer Support?

We are committed to providing excellent customer support. If you have any questions, issues, or requests, there are several ways to get in touch with us. You can email us anytime at support@techshield.com. Additionally, we have a live chat on our website that is available during business hours. If you prefer to speak with one of our customer service representatives, you can give us a call at (12) 3465-9048. Our phone support hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Furthermore, we have a Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) section on our website where you can find answers to common questions. Whatever the issue, we are here to help and strive to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible.

How can I manage my user account?

Managing your user account with us is simple. After logging into our website using your email and password, you will have access to your personal user area. There, you can view and update your personal information, change your password and contact preferences, check the status of your recent orders, and view your order history. If you forgot your password, click on the "Forgot your password?" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it. If you wish to delete your account, please contact our customer support for assistance. Please note that deleting your account will remove all account details and order history from our systems. If you have any issues or questions regarding your user account, please don't hesitate to contact us.

How can I find information about your products and services?

We offer a wide range of products and services, and all relevant information can be found directly on our website. Each product on our site has its own dedicated page, which includes details such as description, specifications, images, price, and availability information. If you are looking for a specific product, you can use the search function on our website to quickly locate it. Additionally, we categorize our products and services to make navigation easier. If you have any questions about a specific product or service, or if you need assistance in choosing the right product for your needs, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support. We are always here to help and provide all the information you need to make an informed purchase.

How do coupons and discounts work?

Periodically, we offer special coupons and discounts to our customers. These may include percentage discounts, cash discounts, or free shipping. To use a coupon, you need to enter the coupon code in the appropriate field during checkout. The discount will be applied automatically to your shopping cart. Please note that only one coupon can be used per order and each coupon has an expiration date, after which it cannot be used. Additionally, some coupons may have specific conditions, such as a minimum purchase value or being valid only for certain products or categories. All conditions and restrictions will be clearly indicated with the coupon. If you encounter any issues while using a coupon or if you have any questions about our discount offers, please don't hesitate to contact us.

How is my information protected?

The security of your information is extremely important to us. We employ various measures to protect the security of your personal information both online and offline. Online, we utilize SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption on all web pages where personal information is required. This security protocol encrypts the information you send through our website to ensure that your transaction is secure. Offline, we implement multiple security measures in our physical data centers to safeguard against the loss, misuse, or alteration of your information. Additionally, we limit access to personal information to employees who need it to perform their job functions. They are required to uphold the confidentiality of such information and face disciplinary penalties, including termination, if they fail to do so. If you have any questions or concerns about security, please don't hesitate to contact us.

How can I leave feedback or review a product?

Your feedback is extremely important to us and to other customers. To leave a review or feedback on a product, visit the product page and click on the "Write a Review" link. You will be prompted to rate the product on a scale of 1 to 5 stars and provide a comment. Your comment can include details about the product's quality, performance, and whether it met your expectations. Please avoid including personal information in your review. All reviews go through a moderation process to ensure they adhere to our review guidelines. If you encounter any issues while leaving a review or if you have any feedback about your shopping experience, please don't hesitate to contact us.
